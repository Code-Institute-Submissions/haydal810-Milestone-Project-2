Choose an Animal?


How to Play:
To begin, press the Green Button to make an animal sound. Match the Sound to the correct Animal Picture, by clicking on the picture you think matches the sound.
Each correct answer scores 1 point. There are 5 rounds.
Good Luck and Enjoy!
